# Enclave (Unified Fortification Mod for Luanti/Minetest)

An advanced, unified fortification and tactical defense mod for **Luanti (Minetest)**. Combining the historical architecture of the Berlin Wall, industrial security of barbed wire fences, and tactical hostile mob behaviors, **Enclave** allows players to generate a massive, fully fortified, closed-loop border base that dynamically adapts to world terrain.

Authored by **ronrob-lu**.

---

## Features

- 🏗️ **Adaptive Base Generation**: Placing the **Enclave Generator Core** triggers the construction of a massive $244 \times 244$ block base centered on the core.
- ⛰️ **Contour-Adaptive Snapping**: Fences, straight concrete walls, and perimeter lines snap exactly to the local terrain heights (including seabed surfaces underwater) column-by-column, ensuring no gaps or floating blocks on hills or valleys.
- 🏰 **Flat Watchtowers**: Watchtowers generate perfectly flat at their center ground coordinates, dynamically building concrete/sandstone foundations downwards to bridge any gaps on slopes.
- 🌿 **Automatic Foliage Excavation**: Clears column vegetation, trees, and dirt from `gy + 1` up to `gy + 25` prior to building, preventing plants or tree leaves from clipping through the fortifications.
- 🛡️ **Guarded Patrol Corridor**: Hostile perimeter guards spawn inside the boundary corridors, patrolling the perimeter while being actively barred and pushed back from entering the central courtyard.
- 🔄 **Facedir Auto-Orientation**: Circular concrete pipe tops (`grenzmauer_top`) and barbed wire fences align automatically to player facing direction or rotated segment orientations.
- 🔗 **Backward Compatibility**: Fully registers compatibility aliases for `berlin_wall:`, `barbwire_fence:`, and `estado_totalitario:`, ensuring old maps, schematics, and configs load seamlessly.

## Enclave Base Architecture & Zoning

The base generated by the **Enclave Core** forms a square perimeter spanning exactly $244 \times 244$ blocks. The coordinates are calculated relative to the placement center $(dX, dZ)$:

```mermaid
graph TD
    subgraph Enclave Zoning Layout (Top-Down View)
    Outer["Outer Perimeter (X/Z = ±122)<br>Barbwire & Chainlink Fences"] 
    --> Corridor["Guarded Corridor (92 <= |dX|, |dZ| <= 121)<br>Hostile NPC Patrols, Watchtowers"]
    --> Inner["Inner Wall (X/Z = ±92)<br>Concrete Grenzmauer 75 Wall"]
    --> Courtyard["Peaceful Central Courtyard (-91 <= dX, dZ <= 91)<br>Safe Zone (No Mob Spawns)"]
    end
    style Courtyard fill:#a3e635,stroke:#4d7c0f,stroke-width:2px;
    style Corridor fill:#f87171,stroke:#b91c1c,stroke-width:2px;
    style Outer fill:#94a3b8,stroke:#475569,stroke-width:2px;
    style Inner fill:#f59e0b,stroke:#d97706,stroke-width:2px;
```

### 1. Peaceful Central Courtyard ($[-91, 91]$)
A safe zone for building, storage, and player farming. Mobs are prohibited from spawning here, and patrolling guards will be actively pushed back into the corridor if they attempt to trespass.

### 2. Inner Wall ($X/Z = \pm 92$)
Built out of three-block-high concrete wall blocks topped with the circular **Grenzmauer 75** concrete pipe.

### 3. Guarded Patrol Corridor ($92 \le |dX/dZ| \le 121$)
A $30$-block wide corridor between the inner wall and outer fence. Watchtowers are placed periodically in this corridor. Mobs spawn initially at bookshelf positions inside the towers and patrol this corridor exclusively.

### 4. Outer Fence ($X/Z = \pm 122$)
Built out of two-block-high chainlink fence topped with barbed wire strands.

## Hostile NPCs & Perimeter Mobs (`enclave:npc`)

The mod introduces hostile border patrol units registered under `enclave:npc` (with compatibility alias `estado_totalitario:npc` supported).

### Core Mechanics:
1. **Bookshelf Spawning**: The generator maps bookshelf locations inside watchtower structures, replaces them with air, and spawns the perimeter guards exactly at these coordinates during base initialization.
2. **Boundary Patrol AI**: When not engaged with a player, guards select patrol destinations strictly inside their assigned corridor side (East, West, North, or South).
3. **Player Aggroness**: Guards have a sight range of $30$ blocks. They actively pursue and attack players on sight.
4. **Physically Contained**: Mobs cannot clip or pass through the concrete walls. If they somehow cross past the inner wall boundary ($|dX/dZ| < 92$) or outside the outer fence ($|dX/dZ| > 121$), the server step script clamps their position and pushes them back into the corridor.
5. **Server Spawn Limits**: Spawning stops permanently once $1000$ kills have occurred across the server. Admins can reset this tracking table.

## Admin Commands

- `/reset_estado`: Resets the total server-wide kill counter to `0` and re-enables mob spawning. Requires `ban` or `server` privileges.

## Installation

1. Copy the `enclave` mod folder into your Luanti/Minetest `mods/` directory.
2. Enable `enclave` in your world settings (`world.mt` or through the game menu).
3. (Optional) In creative mode, obtain the Core block via:
   ```text
   /giveme enclave:enclave
   ```
4. Place the core on the ground, wait for the emerge callback to complete, and watch the Enclave rise!
